About Triloom

Where tradition meets contemporary elegance in every thread and every sparkle

Under the Triloom family, we bring you two collections close to our hearts. Triloom Apparels reflects our love for classic Indian ethnic wear, while Tripi Collections is all about adding a modern sparkle through contemporary jewellery.
